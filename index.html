Welcome

About the Institution

Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering , a premier self-financing engineering college, was started in the year 1985 and is managed by Sri Venkateswara Educational and Health Trust. All the courses run by the college are approved by the Tamil Nadu Government and AICTE and affiliated to Anna University. The College is accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council with good grading. The College is situated in a quiet environment about 37 km from Chennai on the Chennai - Bangalore highway (NH 4) in an area of about 100 acres at Pennalur, Sriperumbudur. The Department of Information Technology has well qualified faculty and is running undergraduate courses in Information Technology and two postgraduate courses M.E Computer Science and Engineering with Specialization in Networks. M.Tech Cyber Forensics and Information Security . This is the first of its kind in Indian Universities. Also, the Department is recognized as Research Centre by Anna University and many scholars are pursuing their research under the same. B.Tech Information Technology has been accredited by National Board of Accreditation for 5 years till 2021. Also, SVCE has been accredited as a National Resource Centre under National Cyber Security Programme (NCSP) by National Cyber Safety & Security Standards. This resource centre is managed by the department of Information Technology.

About the Department

In 1996, Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering pioneered the introduction of the B.Tech degree programme in Information Technology under the affiliation of University of Madras. This is the first of its kind in Indian Universities. The department fulfills the requirements for the award of B.Tech Degree of Anna University. The Venture was initiated under the guidance of our patron Dr.A.C.Muthiah and with the blessings of the Kanchi Paramacharya. When we started, the batch size was 30. This increased to 60 in 1998 and scaled up to 120 by the year 2000. Our growing numbers is indicative of the highly qualified and extremely dedicated teaching faculty of the department who strive for excellence in every sphere of their expertise. It is the constant endeavor of the department to be in touch with changing needs of the IT Industry so as to be responsive in terms of modifications and introduction of new courses to adapt to these technological changes.

About the Conference

Over the past years there have been numerous efforts towards a better understanding of the technology of communications and informatics. Rapid changes have taken place and a host lot of new algorithms and ideas have been proposed. Engineers and scientists have made significant contributions to these fields. The conference will bring together researchers and engineers with different perspectives on these areas. This National Conference provides • A platform where scientists and technologists present, observe and share their latest research results and ideas that will be relevant for the future. • A foundation for expanding Research initiatives. • A launch pad to exhibit the latest research concepts.

Who Can Attend ?

The Conference is intended for the research scholars, faculty and PG students of Engineering Colleges with relevant background.
